ACADEMIC WRITING SKILLS
ORGANIZING SENTENCES INTO A PARAGRAPH

In written English, sentences are organized into paragraphs. A paragraph is a group of sentences about the same topic.

The "Hamburger" Paragraph Structure
1. The Topic Sentence (T)
主题句：描述这一段落主要讲什么。通常是段落的第一句。
2. Supporting Sentences (S)
支持句：对主题进行更多阐述。提供细节 (details) 和 例子 (examples)。位于段落中间。
3. The Concluding Sentence (C)
结尾句：结束段落。总结主要观点。通常以 "In conclusion", "In summary" 或 "To summarize" 开头。
