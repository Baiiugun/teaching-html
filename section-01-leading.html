Grammar Focus · when vs. while

while

+ continuous action (was doing)

While she was walking, she noticed...

= the background / stage

when

+ continuous OR instant action

When they saw the wave, they ran.

= the trigger / moment

while = the stage.   when = what happens on that stage.
